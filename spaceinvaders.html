A recreation of the classic arcade game Space Inavaders
using Functional Reactive Programming (FRP) techniques.

Shoot as many aliens as you can to eliminate them and score points!
Be careful to not let their bullets touch you, or to let the aliens reach the bottom of the screen - this will cause you to die.
To win, shoot every alien on screen. The game restarts once you win or lose.
Orange aliens = 30 points, yellow = 20 points, white = 10 points
You also have 4 shields which dissapear after being hit by bullets 10 times (by either you or the aliens)

Move using the left and right arrow keys, and fire bullets using the space key.
